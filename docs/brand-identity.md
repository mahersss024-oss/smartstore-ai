# SmartStore AI Brand Identity

## Brand Idea

SmartStore AI is a commerce operating system for modern stores. The identity should feel intelligent, operational, trustworthy, and fast without looking playful or generic.

## Logo

- Primary mark: `public/brand/smartstore-mark.svg`
- Horizontal logo: `public/brand/smartstore-logo-horizontal.svg`
- Use the horizontal logo in Clerk, emails, documents, and wide layouts.
- Use the mark for favicons, app icons, small buttons, and square placements.

## Colors

- Deep navy: `#0F172A`
- Store green: `#14532D`
- Operational cyan: `#0E7490`
- AI mint: `#9FFFD2`
- Surface: `#F8FDFF`
- Text: `#0F172A`
- Muted text: `#475569`

## Voice

Use direct operational language:

- Arabic: تشغيل ذكي للمتاجر
- English: AI-powered commerce operations
- Short descriptor: Commerce OS

Avoid vague startup phrases. Prefer clear product language around products, orders, customers, payments, delivery, and automation.

## Clerk Uploads

- Logo: upload `public/brand/smartstore-logo-horizontal.png`.
- Favicon: upload `public/brand/smartstore-mark-512.png` or `public/favicon.ico`.

The Clerk email sender/application name must still be changed from Clerk Dashboard to `SmartStore AI`.
